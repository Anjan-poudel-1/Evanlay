About US

How We Grew As A Company

Evenlay was formed in 2010 with the aim to mordernize architechture, build a better and happy living society. The company was initiated as a small project with 4 graduates trying to understand the possibilities and difficulties of modern architechture in an unplanned,unmanaged urban area like Kathmandu. Ten years after, we are a successful buissness expanding all over Nepal and we plan to further expand in South-east Asia.

Growing businesses face a range of challenges. As a business grows, different problems and opportunities demand different solutions - what worked a year ago might now be not the best approach. All too often, avoidable mistakes turn what could have been a great business into an also-ran.

The Power Of Design To Create A Better World

Our Mission is simple, Making Built spaces that enrich lives and inspire people to meet the vision we have of making world, architecturally a better place to live. Buildings are not just ugly boxes to live in. It can be much broader, larger. It can ignite, the creative spree into the people around with its sheer status of just being there, inviting, inspiring and motivating.

As we’ve developed, we’ve always stood at the forefront of design & Construction, adopting latest computer application, tools & methodology.

Clients Come First In Everything We Do

Through the years, we’ve worked tirelessly to refine our approach to deliver exceptional results for our clients. We’re dedicated to providing professional design services that fulfill our clients’ needs, according to their budget and schedule parameters and provide viable solutions that create functional spaces within enriched aesthetic environments.

We provide innovative design solutions implemented using state-of-the-art technology throughout all phases of our design services. And we’re still continually refining our process to deliver exceptional designs that lead to on-time, on-budget projects.
